# StuCode

StuCode is a comprehensive platform for publishing programming articles, designed to foster collaboration and learning among developers of all levels. With a variety of tools and features, StuCode enables users to create content, follow others, join communities, participate in discussions, and attend events. 🚀🖋️💻

## Features 🛠️

- **Article Creation and Publishing**: Write and publish articles with a rich text editor and code syntax highlighting. ✍️📝🎨
- **User Following**: Follow your favorite authors to receive notifications about their new publications. 📚🔔
- **Communities**: Join and collaborate in communities based on your tech interests. 🤝🌐
- **Forums**: Engage in discussions, resolve queries, and share knowledge in specialized forums. 💬🔍
- **Private Messaging**: Connect directly with other users for collaborations and inquiries. ✉️👥
- **Comments and Feedback**: Allow readers to leave comments and provide constructive feedback on articles. 💬📝
- **Profile Customization**: Personalize your profile with a bio, social links, skills, and work experience. 📌👤
- **Tags and Categories**: Articles can be tagged and categorized for easy search and discovery. 🔖📂
- **Advanced Search**: Filter articles by tags, categories, author, publication date, and more. 🔍📅
- **External Tool Integration**: Integrate with external tools like GitHub, Stack Overflow, and social media for sharing articles. 🛠️🔄
- **Real-time Notifications**: Receive real-time notifications for new publications, comments, and private messages. 📬🕒
- **Article Analytics**: Analytics tools for authors to view detailed statistics on their articles' performance. 📊📈
- **Article Rating System**: Users can rate articles to highlight the most valuable content. ⭐👍

## Technologies Used 💻

StuCode leverages the latest technologies to deliver a robust and scalable solution:

- Frontend: `React`
- Backend: `Node.js`, `Express.js`
- Database: `MongoDB`, `PostgreSQL`
